Generate

Rough plots in, seamless worlds out.

Block out a plot in the editor and worldsplat grows it into one continuous gaussian ground.

  • Add plots as you go — seams blend automatically across tiles.
  • Reshape after generation: raise, lower, or move any plot live.
  • Objects stay editable; the ground stays one unified splat.
